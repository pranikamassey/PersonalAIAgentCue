1. Load the extension

Open chrome://extensions
Toggle Developer mode on (top-right)
Click Load unpacked
Select the folder: /Users/pranika/Documents/Claude/Projects/ProjectAgent/build/chrome-mv3-dev
You should see "Buddy" appear in the list with your blue icon
2. Pin and open it

Click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar → pin Buddy
Click the Buddy icon → side panel opens on the right
3. Add your API key

In the side panel header, click Settings (top-right)
Paste your sk-ant-... key from https://console.anthropic.com
Click Save settings
4. Try each feature

Chat tab — type "hi" and hit Send. You should get a Claude reply. The character emoji switches to 🤔 while thinking.
Tabs tab — shows all your open tabs grouped by domain
Gmail tab — open mail.google.com in another tab, wait ~2s, then come back; threads should appear
Social tab — visit twitter/reddit/youtube/etc, come back and watch the bars fill
Summarize page button in Chat — navigate to any article, click it
Triage Gmail button in Chat — after Gmail tab has data
